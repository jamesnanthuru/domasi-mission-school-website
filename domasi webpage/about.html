About Domasi Mission Secondary School

Excellence in Education, Faith, and Service since 1994

Our Story

Domasi Mission Secondary School was established in 1994 and is located in the Zomba District of Malawi's Southern Region. It is one of six secondary schools under the Blantyre Synod Education Department, rooted in a tradition of academic excellence and holistic development.

The school is situated 4 km from Songani Market along the M8 road, making it accessible to students from across the region. Since its founding, Domasi Mission has grown into a premier institution known for producing top-performing students in national examinations.

We offer both boarding and day scholar education for both girls and boys, providing a conducive learning environment that nurtures spiritual, physical, social, and intellectual growth.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, equitable, relevant, and holistic education through qualified, motivated teachers and effective resource use, developing productive citizens for Malawi.

Our Vision

To achieve single‑digit grades at MSCE while maintaining a 100% pass rate, ensuring efficient and holistic education for Malawi's transformation.

Our Academic Programs

We offer a comprehensive curriculum leading to the Malawi School Certificate of Education (MSCE). Below are the 12 subjects taught at Domasi Mission Secondary School:

Agriculture Biology Bible Knowledge Business Studies Chemistry Chichewa English Geography History Mathematics Physics Social Studies / Life Skills

In addition to these core subjects, students participate in co‑curricular activities including sports, music, and community service.
