About Our School

Domasi Mission Secondary School is a prominent institution in Zomba District, part of the Blantyre Synod Education Department. Renowned for academic excellence, we consistently rank among the top 10 in MSCE performance nationwide.

Our holistic education develops spiritual, physical, social, and intellectual capabilities, producing productive citizens for Malawi.

Read More

Our Mission

To provide accessible, equitable, relevant, and holistic education through qualified, motivated teachers and effective resource use, developing productive citizens.

Our Vision

To achieve single‑digit grades at MSCE while maintaining a 100% pass rate, ensuring efficient and holistic education for Malawi's transformation.

Core Values

Hardwork · Excellency · Transparency · Accountability · Integrity · Collaboration · Role Modelling
